A bit about this website and this d3 Interactive Map.


Make the world a better place!


This website and interactive map was created as part of a lab from Geography 575 at University of Wisconsin Madison. The primary outcomes were the following.

-->Use the D3 library to coordinate interactions across multiple visualizations.
-->Identify the GeoJSON and TopoJSON formats.
-->Implement operator primitives, including pan, zoom, retrieve, and sequence.
-->Implement reexpress and retrieve for coordinated, multivariate visualization.
